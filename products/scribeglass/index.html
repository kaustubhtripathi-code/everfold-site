How it works

Rough notes in. Clean notes out.

01

Jot rough notes

Keywords, half-sentences — whatever you'd scribble during the call. No pressure to be tidy.

02

Capture the audio

Use your mic, or share a tab to hear the other participants. Transcribed on-device with Whisper — no bot, no upload.

03

Enhance with AI

One click merges your notes with the transcript into a structured doc, shaped by a template. Then chat with it.
